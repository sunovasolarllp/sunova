SOLAR TECH GUIDE

Types of Solar Panels in the Market

Understand the different solar photovoltaic panel technologies available to power your home or business.

Monocrystalline PERC

Efficiency: 20% - 22%

Crafted from premium single-crystal silicon. Offers the highest power density, superior low-light performance, and a sleek black aesthetic. Ideal for residential rooftops.

Polycrystalline Cells

Efficiency: 15% - 17%

Constructed by melting multiple raw silicon crystals. Features a traditional blue speckled look. Highly cost-effective and budget-friendly for large ground-mounted arrays.

Bifacial Double-Sided

Efficiency: 22% - 25%

Dual-sided glass structure that absorbs direct sunlight from the front and reflected light (albedo) from the back. Boosts total generation yield by up to 15-25%.

Thin-Film & Flexible

Efficiency: 11% - 13%

Ultra-thin, lightweight, and bendable sheets made from Cadmium Telluride or CIGS. Adapts smoothly to curved structures, temporary camping, or caravan roofs.

📋 Required Documents for Solar Bank Loans (Kerala)

To apply for a solar installation loan (SBI, Federal Bank, Canara Bank, etc.), you will generally need to submit:

  • 1. Identification: Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, and 2 passport-sized photos.
  • 2. Property Proofs: Latest Land Tax Receipt (Thandaper) and Building Tax Receipt (Panchayat/Municipality).
  • 3. KSEB Connection: Latest KSEB Electricity Bill (address must match property).
  • 4. Income Proof:
    • Salaried: Last 3 months' salary slips & 6 months' bank statements.
    • Self-employed: Last 2 years' IT Returns & 6 months' bank statements.
  • 5. Technical Quote: Official quote/proforma invoice and MNRE compliance certificate (provided by Sunova Solar LLP).

INTERCONNECTION PROCESS

KSEB Net Metering & Subsidy Flow

How we manage your solar integration with the Kerala State Electricity Board (KSEB) from registration to commissioning.

01

Portal Registration

Sunova Solar LLP registers your system on the KSEB Soura online portal, uploading property ownership records and connection bills.

02

KSEB Feasibility Review

The local KSEB section office inspects the local transformer capacity load and issues the official feasibility clearance approval.

03

Installation & Work Report

Our engineers complete structural solar mounting and electrical cabling. We file the KSEB physical completion report.

04

Net Metering & Savings

KSEB tests the installation, commissions the bidirectional Net-Meter, and activates your KSEB billing discount. Subsidies are sent directly to your bank!
